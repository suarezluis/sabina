About me...

I was born and raised on the Caspian Sea in a small town called Baku in Azerbaijan.

Growing up behind the theater stage with a designer mom, whom I admire and love dearly, taught me how to love and appreciate art. Art took me through a lot of dark days, it is my way of meditating and expressing myself.

Using elaborate patterning and detail, I create embellished costumes with beading and handwork.

I also paint expressionistically with an idea or subject in my mind.

I have traveled and lived in Moscow, Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Istanbul, and Cambodia; now living between two of my favorite cities Austin and New York.
